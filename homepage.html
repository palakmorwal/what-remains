"WHAT REMAINS"

Sometimes I try to picture moments I can no longer experience. What remains are fragments, half-remembered and half-invented, stitched together by time and longing. This project is a quiet attempt to look back through those pieces, to see what still reflects, and what has already faded.

This digital archive serves a purpose of preserving some of my childhood memories that did not get lost in my father's hard drive and i intend to put them out as a digital album for myself.
